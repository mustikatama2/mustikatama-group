About the Group

Built in East Java, Operating Globally

Mustikatama Group is a diversified industrial group headquartered in Lumajang, East Java ‚Äî one of Indonesia's most productive regions for both agriculture and timber resources.

Founded in 1987, the Group has grown from its origins in agricultural processing into a multi-sector industrial enterprise, with manufacturing capabilities across timber panel production, wood-working and agricultural commodity processing.

Today, the Group operates three business units: PT Mustika Buana Sejahtera (MBS), PT Mustika Bahana Jaya (MBJ), and PT Karya Setya Mustikatama (KSMT) ‚Äî each a specialist in its field, sharing the Group's commitment to quality, reliability and sustainable operations.

Group History

35+ Years of Industrial Growth

1987

Mustikatama Group Founded

Group established in Lumajang, East Java with initial operations in salt iodization and agricultural processing.

1996

PT Mustika Bahana Jaya (MBJ) Established

Timber operations expand with MBJ specialising in Finger-Joint Laminated Board from Rubberwood, Merkusii Pine and Albizia Falcata.

2006

PT Mustika Buana Sejahtera (MBS) Established

MBS founded as a dedicated plywood and blockboard manufacturer, leveraging the Group's accumulated timber expertise in East Java.

2009

MBS Achieves CARB Phase II Certification

A landmark milestone ‚Äî MBS becomes CARB Phase II certified, opening access to the US market and setting the standard for Group quality.

Today

1,000+ Workers ‚Äî 5 Continents

The Group operates across timber panels and agricultural processing, supplying buyers in Japan, USA, Europe, the Americas, Africa and Oceania.
