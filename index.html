About the Group

Mustikatama Group is a diversified industrial group headquartered in Lumajang, East Java, Indonesia. Founded in 1989, the Group has grown across two sectors — timber & wood-working and agricultural processing — through three specialist business units: MBS, MBJ and KSMT.

All operations are based in Lumajang, leveraging the region's natural resources, agricultural productivity and logistics infrastructure. The Group employs over 1,500 workers combined and serves buyers across Asia, the Americas, Europe, Africa and Oceania.
